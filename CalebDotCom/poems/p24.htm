The Weight of Waiting

Here’s to the in-betweens.
The not yets and not quites
The toddlers and the tweens
Opening sets and long nights
Here’s to the waiting
The moments just sitting
While fate is baiting
Stay.
Just stay.
Don’t give up and go
Or find another way
I want you to know
To wait for your day
Waiting is real
And don’t run away
From the feelings you feel
The excitement.
The tension
All of that suspension.
Hold on to those
Cause that is what life is about
Not the big shows
When you sing, dance and shout.
No
It’s about anticipation
The frustration
The moments before
That are left at the door
Here's to those.
The seconds of normal
Before we’re formal
When no one knows
Yet.
Stay there
Pause before
Stand on shore
Hold the stare.
Don’t chase fate.
Wait.
