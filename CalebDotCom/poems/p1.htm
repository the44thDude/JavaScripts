A greeting to the pilgrim


Welcome to the nothing.
Welcome to the desert.
Welcome to this sparse place
Where your thirst is never quenched
Where you strive across the land.
Where you can't walk or move without slipping
And falling in the sand.

Welcome to the nothing
Welcome to the sea
Welcome to this dark place
Where your breaths are always labored
Where you strive to never drown
Where you can't cough or gasp without sinking
And falling deeper down

Welcome to the nothing
Welcome to the prison
Welcome to this hard place
Where your steps are always numbered
Where you strive to never break
Where you can't talk or smile without fighting
And falling for their sake.

Welcome to the nothing, where everyone talks but nobody speaks
Welcome to the nothing where everyone looks but nobody sees.
Welcome to the nothing where everyone hears but nobody listens
Welcome
Welcome
Welcome
Because the nothing is not all there is.
There is no desert without mountains, no thirst without water, and no striving without a goal
There is no sea without islands, no drowning without air, and no striving without hope.
There is no prison without freedom, no fighting without bravery, and no striving without success.
So welcome to the nothing because that is where you are.
but the nothing just means, something isn't very far.
