The View from Here

I stand atop a peak where I can see my past
I’ve walked till I was weak, and tried to go fast
I look back on my journey and think through my trail.
I’ve done much learning I learn whenever I fail.

There have been valleys, it has been hard.
There have been rocks, it has been slow.
what would mountains be without valleys? What is darkness without light?
Life would have no color, and the views wouldn't be worth the height.

I think of the forks, the times I chose wrong.
I relied on my own works and didn’t last long.
I took paths of darkness, and had to be lead to light
I often felt helpless and was made to stand and fight

There have been hopes it has been fun.
There have been views it has been pretty.
Where will I go? What awaits me?
The only way to know is to walk on and see.
