Unchained:

Freedom is strange,
It gives us range
To make life change,
And yet this treat
Is bittersweet.
We crave independence
Yet see no hindrance
To the way we relate
The power to change fate
To wipe clean a dirty slate
Gives us glee.
We are free
But the price for this
Is loneliness.
What's that worth?
We go forth,
From death to birth,
Don't look back,
On our track,
Make our own way,
But we don't stay,
And so we pay.
