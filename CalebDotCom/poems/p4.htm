Do as We are Told:

Do as we are told. Do as we must
We have to listen even without trust.
We must blindly step and follow the path
Or face what might happen, the aftermath
We are destined to repeat and destined to follow in the footsteps and footfalls of those who went before us. Destined to mirror and mimic the prints in the dust to pursue what we must.
We are destined to do what the world would have us. Destined to pursue even if our minds are against it.
Or at least we have to seem as we are. Act so that the voice speaking out and fish going upstream isn't noticed. or the crowd would crush it under the pretense of safety.
We are destined to act in the way we are told
From when we are young to when we grow old
From this fate we cannot defend
Because we are fated to pretend.
