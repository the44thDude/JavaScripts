Each second counts

As time does fly
All of us cry
“Slow down, don't pass us by”
This backwards plea
Is wrong you see
For time is but a currency
Each second has weight
Like the coins of fate
To be spent on love or hate
We spend forevers
And promise Nevers
But leave none for good endeavors
We spend and spend
But to no end
And don't expect life’s sad trend
We think of time
And the clock’s chime
As a path that we must climb
But this lie
Is the reason why
It seems to us that time does fly.
