Sounds in the dark

I remember two gates
One was wide and clear.
The path was flat.
And the destination near.
The other I almost missed.
It was small and narrow
And the path did duck and twist

Now it’s an echo, one of many bouncing and spinning all around
In the darkness and pain all I hear are echoes, echoes are the only sound.

I remember being led
Called and guided I was pulled.
I joined the flock and was loved
And when I strayed, he held me from the cold
He walked me and pushed me
I was tempted and he always welcomed me back
And in my fear,he shushed me.

Now he is and echo, one of many bouncing and spinning all around
In the darkness and pain all I hear are echoes, echoes are the only sound.

I remember a cross,
at the top of the Calvary hill
I asked him what it was for
And he said it was meant to kill
I shied in fear and to him looked
He smiled in kindness, nodded
And went up the hill to the cross and my place took.

Now its and echo, one of many bouncing and spinning all around
In the darkness and pain all I hear are echoes, echoes are the only sound.

I remember a woman
Her lips were honey
And her speech smooth as oil and
She led me to a path that was sunny.
But she soon grew bitter
The path wandered and wormwood grew and
Her face grew dull and lost its glitter.

Now she is and echo, one of many bouncing and spinning all around
In the darkness and pain all I hear are echoes, echoes are the only sound.

I remember a mountain’s path
On one side was a cliff
I tried to pass it quickly running
But the way was slick and my feet stiff
I didn’t slip, it was worse
My steps were wrong
And I flew off my course

Now it’s an echo, one of many bouncing and spinning all around
In the darkness and pain all I hear are echoes, echoes are the only sound.
I cry out but hear only echoes
The sounds bounce back at me
The loneliness fills me
The darkness swallows me
The pain is all I feel

“Why have you forsaken me?”
