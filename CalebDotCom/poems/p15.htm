Rain:

Dripping and dropping,
Cleaning and cleansing
Pooling and plopping
It falls. Wetting and washing
Washing the dirt
Washing the dust
Washing all the world of all the stuff
The stuff that was slung
And flung, plopping
And now the fish are happy
Swimming flipping and flopping
The cats are not happy
Hissing and straining
As though each drop is paining
My shoes are squelching
My feet are squeaking
And the water is draining
Oh joy, it must be raining
