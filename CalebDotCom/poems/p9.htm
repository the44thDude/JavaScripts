Hardship:

Breath shortens, eyes dilate, and pulse quickens.
Thoughts speed by blurring together until they are silent.
A moment of calm, The world waits, and there, in the quiet,
Sweat drips.
It seems impossible, the trial is too hard
The hill too steep, the fear too complete.
Every step is agony and every breath is a gift to cherish.

Time lengthens, muscles tense, and sound fades.
Thoughts fade behind reflex until instinct is the only guide.
A moment of fury, the world gasps, and there, in the frenzy
Blood drips.
It seems impossible the pain is too great
The rocks too sharp the weakness too complete.
Every breath is agony and every feeling a gift to cherish

Space tilts, breath catches, pain rages.
Thoughts slow and come into focus until reality returns.
A moment of fear, the world laughs and there, in the pain,
Tears drip.
It seemed too impossible, the trial was too hard.
The rocks too steep, but now it is complete
Every feeling is agony, and life a gift to cherish
